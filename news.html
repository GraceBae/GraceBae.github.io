Recent News

Congratulations to Tongze Zhang on the acceptance of his paper in JMIR Human Factors.
“Emotion-Adaptive LLM-Driven Clinical Decision Support: User Evaluation of the Empathic-CDSS Framework for Trust and Explainability” was accepted in JMIR Human Factors on March 16, 2026. This work advances real-time, emotion-aware, and explainable AI approaches to improve trust and usability in clinical decision support systems.
