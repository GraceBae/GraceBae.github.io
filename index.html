Building Human-Centered AI Systems for Health, Emotion, and Well-being

The CARE AI Lab develops emotionally intelligent, explainable, and privacy-preserving AI systems that combine mobile sensing, affective computing, and human-AI interaction to support health, resilience, and digital well-being.

Explore Research Join the Lab

Research Areas

Our research focuses on emotionally intelligent, explainable, and privacy-preserving AI systems for healthcare, mental health, digital therapeutics, and human-centered decision support.

Affective AI

Emotion-aware AI systems that sense, interpret, and respond to human affective states.

Digital Health

Mobile and wearable sensing systems for real-world health monitoring and intervention.

Explainable AI

Transparent AI models that support trust, clinical reasoning, and human-centered decisions.

Human-AI Interaction

Designing AI systems that collaborate with people in meaningful, ethical, and usable ways.

Featured Projects

FacePsy

Smartphone-based facial behavior sensing for mental health monitoring in natural environments.

Empathic Clinical Decision Support

Emotion-adaptive AI systems that combine explainability, causal reasoning, and human-centered design.

Digital Therapeutics for Recovery

AI-driven systems for resilience, emotion regulation, and personalized support in substance use recovery.
