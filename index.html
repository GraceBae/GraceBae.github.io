Research Areas

Our research develops human-centered AI systems that sense, explain, and support human health, emotion, behavior, and resilience in real-world contexts.

Emotionally Intelligent AI

Building AI systems that recognize, interpret, and respond to affective signals from facial behavior, language, physiology, and everyday interaction.

Mobile & Wearable Health Sensing

Developing smartphone- and wearable-based sensing frameworks to monitor health, behavior, mood, substance use, sleep, and recovery in natural environments.

Explainable AI for Healthcare

Designing interpretable and actionable AI models that support clinical decision-making, self-understanding, and trust in high-stakes health contexts.

Digital Therapeutics & JITAIs

Creating personalized, just-in-time adaptive interventions to support resilience, emotion regulation, recovery, and long-term well-being.

Human-AI Collaboration

Studying how people interact with AI systems in healthcare, education, and everyday decision-making, with attention to usability, trust, agency, and ethical design.

Responsible & Privacy-Aware AI

Advancing AI systems that are transparent, privacy-conscious, inclusive, and deployable in real-world health and community settings.

Open-Source AI Systems

We develop deployable, privacy-aware, and human-centered AI systems for digital health, affective computing, explainable AI, and mobile sensing.

Evaluation-First Personalized AI Health Coaching

The NeurIPS MTI LLM framework investigates evaluation-first personalization strategies for multi-turn AI health coaching with real users. The system introduces subgroup-aware offline policy evaluation and adaptive conversational decision modeling to identify personalization benefits and hidden subgroup harms that are often obscured by average performance metrics. This work advances human-centered generative AI by emphasizing explainability, adaptive interaction, and rigorous evaluation for personalized digital health interventions.

SePA

SePA is a search-enhanced personalized AI framework for health coaching that integrates retrieval augmentation, context-aware reasoning, and personalized conversational support. Unlike static LLM-based coaching systems, SePA dynamically incorporates trusted external knowledge sources and adaptive query expansion to provide contextually grounded and user-specific recommendations. The framework advances explainable and personalized health AI by combining retrieval-based intelligence, predictive user modeling, and human-centered conversational interaction.

FacePsy

FacePsy is an open-source affective mobile sensing platform that enables real-world mental health monitoring using smartphone-based facial behavior analysis. Unlike traditional lab-based affective computing systems, FacePsy supports scalable and privacy-aware sensing in naturalistic environments by leveraging front-camera facial behavior, head gestures, and multimodal contextual signals. The system advances deployable digital mental health research by integrating mobile sensing, explainable AI, and human-centered design for depression and behavioral health applications.

PupilSense

PupilSense is a deep learning–based mobile pupillometry framework designed for detecting depressive states through smartphone-captured pupillary responses in everyday environments. The system addresses a major limitation of traditional pupillometry research by enabling scalable, real-world affective sensing outside controlled laboratory settings. By combining computer vision, behavioral sensing, and mental health modeling, PupilSense advances privacy-aware and accessible approaches for continuous mood and behavioral monitoring.
